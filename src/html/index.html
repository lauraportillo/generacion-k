Nekgikis V tomb

Heading to expedition k

In 2020, the architect Laurix Portillo began to study a new mysterious language called Javascript. Her intention was none other than to be part of the famous K expedition and start her career as a developer. This expedition would take her to ancient Egypt, where the archaeological remains of the tomb of Pharaoh Nekgikis V had just been discovered, which stood out for being a Opportunity-giving pharaoh to Egyptian junior developers 3000 years ago. A mysterious encrypted message needed to be solved: a phrase and a series of numbers raised the suspicions of archaeologists. The secret url, would lead us to the solution of the mystery. After long nights of study, Laurix Portillo found the enigma! In this repo, you can access the secret scripts.
